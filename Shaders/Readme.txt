SinCity shaders main purpose is making the scene black&white (as seen on the SincityPalette.png color range is less on grays to create contrastable scenes), since a high spectrum of red channel colors stay still red (more redish than actual)

sincity.rfx can be apply to models
sincity2D.rfx can be apply to textures
